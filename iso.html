ISO

ISO represents the sensitivity ratings for camera sensors.
As you increase your ISO number, your photos will be brighter.
It's a good tool to help you capture images in dark environments or be more flexible about your aperture and shutter speed settings. However, a photo taken at too high of an ISO will show a lot of grain.
